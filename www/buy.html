Where to Buy LaunchPad

You will need a Texas Instruments LaunchPad board in order to use Energia. Luckily, it is very easy to acquire them.

Buy from TI eStore

Order an MSP430, C2000, or Tiva LaunchPad directly from TI. You can see all their LaunchPad products here.
  • MSP430G2
  • C2000 Piccolo
  • Tiva C Series

Buy a LaunchPad board from a Distributor.

Where to Buy BoosterPacks

BoosterPacks allow you to extend the functionality of your LaunchPad Board. There are BoosterPacks built by TI as well as independent companies and designers. You can find a complete list of BoosterPacks and where to purchase them at www.ti.com/boosterpack.
